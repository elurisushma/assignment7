DRY Wall Hanging

Learn how to make beautiful Dry Wall Hanging like these!

Materials:

    Wooden dowel or a sturdy stick
    Yarn or string (various colors)
    Scissors
    Beads, feathers, or any decorative items (optional)
    Glue (optional)

Instructions:

Step 1: Prepare the Wooden Dowel/Stick

Choose a wooden dowel or a stick. The length depends on how wide you want your wall hanging. You can paint it or leave it natural depending on your style.

Step 2: Cut the Yarn

Decide on the length of your hanging. Cut multiple strands of yarn in different lengths for a layered look. Use different colors of yarn for added vibrancy.

Step 3: Tie the Yarn

Take a piece of yarn, fold it in half, and create a loop at the fold. Place the loop under the dowel, pull the loose ends through the loop, and tighten. Repeat this process.

Step 4: Add Beads or Feathers (Optional)

Thread beads or tie feathers to some strands of yarn for extra detail. Secure with a knot or glue.

Step 5: Trim and Shape

Once all the yarn is attached, trim the bottom to your desired shape: straight, diagonal, or even in a "V" shape.

Step 6: Hang It Up

Tie a piece of yarn or string to each end of the dowel to create a hanging loop. Your DIY wall hanging is ready to be displayed!
