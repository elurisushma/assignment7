How to Make These Beautiful Paper Flowers

    Materials Needed: Colored paper, scissors, glue, floral wire, and tape.
    Step 1: Cut the paper into squares or rectangles based on the flower type.
    Step 2: Fold the paper and cut it into petal shapes.
    Step 3: Shape the petals and glue them to form the flower.
    Step 4: Attach floral wire or a pipe cleaner as the stem.
    Step 5: Optionally, add leaves or a paper center to finish the flower.
